Website Landing Page Content – First Page
Company Name: Fight From Diseases
Industry: Medical Tourism

1. Hero Section – Medical Tourism Visual
Full-width hero banner showcasing international patients receiving advanced medical care in India. The
visual should instantly convey trust, affordability, and world-class healthcare. A primary 'Contact Us'
call-to-action button should open a pop-up Google Form.

2. About Us – Mission & Vision
Mission: To connect international patients with trusted hospitals, renowned doctors, and affordable
world-class treatments in India, ensuring a seamless and compassionate medical journey.
Vision: To become a globally recognized medical tourism facilitator known for ethical practices,
patient-centric care, and long-term international healthcare partnerships.

3. Partner Hospitals
A dedicated section displaying logos of partner multi-speciality and super-speciality hospitals across India.
This section should build credibility and trust.

A visually engaging photo gallery featuring hospital infrastructure, doctors, international patients, treatment
environments, and recovery care. Images should open in a lightbox view.

6. Contact Information
Location: India
Serving patients from 20+ countries
Email, Phone, and WhatsApp details to be added. A floating WhatsApp contact icon is recommended for

An attractive pop-up Google Form should be accessible across the website via CTA buttons.

The website should maintain a premium healthcare look using a clean palette, modern
